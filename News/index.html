2025 Ford F-150

The 2025 Ford F-150 is a full-size pickup truck that combines robust performance, advanced technology, and practical design. It offers a range of powerful engine options, including a standard 2.7L EcoBoost® V6 delivering 325 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque, and an available 5.0L V8 producing 400 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque, catering to various performance needs. With its blend of power, comfort, and innovative features, the 2025 Ford F-150 continues to be a compelling choice for those seeking a reliable and capable pickup truck.
